## IMPORTANT: CLUB vs IEEE STUDENT MEMBERSHIP (get this right every time)
There are TWO separate things. Always make this distinction clear in all materials:

### 1. Club Membership (FREE)
- Joining the IEEE Trevecca club costs nothing
- Any Trevecca student can join for free
- Club members can attend all events, meetings, tours, and activities
- No commitment, no fees, just show up and participate

### 2. IEEE Student Membership ($16/year — 50% off, limited time)
- This is OPTIONAL — not required to be in the club
- Regular price is $32/year, currently 50% off at $16/year
- This is a GLOBAL IEEE membership, not just our club
- Any student interested in IEEE benefits can sign up — not limited to club members
- Benefits of IEEE Student Membership include:
  - Join a global community of 420,000+ technology and engineering professionals
  - Access to IEEE Xplore — the largest library of electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics technical literature
  - Networking with professionals in your area and technical interests
  - Mentorship from professional engineers and technologists
  - Get involved in IEEE standards development
  - Stay current on the latest technology trends, industry news, and events
  - IEEE email alias (no yet) to enhance professional image
  - Discounts on IEEE conferences, events, and publications
  - Access to IEEE Potentials Magazine (written for students)
  - Eligibility for IEEE scholarships, awards, and travel grants
  - Career resources including job boards and resume tools
  - Membership that pays for itself through conference and product discounts
  - Credential that stands out on resumes — recognized worldwide

### How to talk about both:
- "Join our club for FREE and come to all our events"
- "Want even more? Become an IEEE Student Member for just $16/year (50% off right now) and unlock global benefits"
- Never make it sound like you MUST pay $16 to participate in the club
