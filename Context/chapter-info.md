- Chapter: New — actively recruiting

## What is IEEE?
IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization. It provides student chapters at universities that give students access to networking, conferences, publications, career resources, and a global community of engineers and technologists.

## What we offer students
- Free club membership (no cost to attend events or join)
- Optional IEEE student membership: $16/year (50% off)
- Tours of Nashville tech companies
- Hands-on tech events and workshops
- Real project experience for resumes
- Officer and leadership positions
- Internship connections through IEEE network
- Community for both technical and non-technical students

## Nashville Tech Scene Context
Nashville has a growing tech ecosystem with companies in healthcare tech, fintech, music tech, and logistics. This gives our chapter a unique advantage — students can connect with real local companies, not just hear about Silicon Valley from a distance.
